Welcome to Nexus, an intelligent genomics AI platform designed to accelerate your genomic analysis and case management workflows. Whether you're exploring genomic data through natural language queries or managing complex patient cases, Nexus combines advanced AI with comprehensive genomic databases to deliver actionable insights.

Nexus empowers you with two flexible operating modes and a suite of genomics tools:

Welcome to Purna, an intelligent genomics AI platform that accelerates your genomic analysis and case management workflows. Combine advanced AI with comprehensive genomic databases to get actionable insights through natural language.

## Key features

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Agent Mode" icon="message-square">
    Chat-first interface for genomics queries. Ask natural language questions about genes, variants, diseases, and clinical implications.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Case Mode" icon="folder-open">
    Manage patient cases with structured data. Track findings, phenotypes, variants, and access control in one workspace.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Variant analysis" icon="dna">
    Browse, filter, and analyze genetic variants with cards and table views. Apply gene panels and phenotype-driven filters.
  </Card>
  <Card title="ACMG classification" icon="clipboard-check">
    AI-enhanced ACMG/AMP variant classification with evidence from ClinVar, PubMed, gnomAD, and functional data sources.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Genomic database search" icon="database">
    Access ClinVar, OMIM, PubMed, gene databases, and SNP repositories. Toggle five categories of research sources.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Clinical reports" icon="file-lines">
    Generate variant interpretation reports, clinical findings summaries, ACMG evidence reports, and case summaries.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Visualizations" icon="chart-bar">
    Interactive charts for genomic data. Pie, bar, line, and area charts generated from natural language requests.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Code execution" icon="code">
    Run Python scripts directly in your browser with pandas, matplotlib, and seaborn. Data never leaves your session.
  </Card>
</Columns>
